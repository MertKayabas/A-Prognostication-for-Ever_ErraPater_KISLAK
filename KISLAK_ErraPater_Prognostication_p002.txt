Bookplate / Ownership Mark

Text on image:

HENRICI CWNLIF FE ARMIGER.
EX LIBRIS

Description:
This page contains a decorative ex libris (bookplate) belonging to Henrici Cunliffe, Armiger (Henry Cunliffe, Esquire). The design features a heraldic dog sitting atop an ornate letter “C”, surrounded by floral motifs and small crosses. It marks an earlier owner of the book.
